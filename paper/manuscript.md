## Methods

- **Source datasets.** Enumerated with licences and provenance in `config/provenance.py`.
- **Unification pipeline.** `BasePipeline` + step catalog (`docs/steps.md`): conversion, id/label/mask
  unification, validation.
- **Conventions.** UMIE id `{dataset_uid}_{modality_id}_{study_id}_{img_id}`; `{uid}_{name}/{modality}/
  Images|Masks/` layout; RadLex label/mask ontologies (`config/labels.py`, `config/masks.py`); JSONL
  metadata (v1 flat, optional v2 hierarchical — `docs/schema.md`).
- **Ontology.** Hierarchical, multi-axis RadLex taxonomy with optional SNOMED/FMA/Uberon cross-codes
  (`config/ontology.py`).
- **3D geometry.** Orientation-preserving slicing + combined NIfTI/PNG output (`src/geometry.py`).
